A Closer Look at Carbon Emissions

When it comes to environmental impact, the science as well as the actions taken are complex. The Paris Agreement signed in December 2015, unified close to 200 countries across the globe to implement carbon reduction. Working to rewire and create a fossil fuel free world, agreeing to keep global temperatures from rising below 2 degrees Celsius. As of April 2021, President Joe Biden announced a goal to cut emissions at the least in half by 2030. Doubling the goal of the 2015 Paris Agreement, signed by Global world leaders including former President Barack Obama. Our researcher has taken the time to start from the very beginning when the term “Carbon Footprint” even came about and what the current administration is doing to combat Climate change. As well as exploring websites that already generate Carbon Footprint. Utilizing this information as a foundation and seeing what they are essentially missing, so Commiting Carbon can be the most effective tracking method.
