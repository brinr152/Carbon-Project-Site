What Are We Doing Now?

As of April 2021, President Joe Biden announced a goal to cut emissions at the least in half by 2030. Doubling the goal of the 2015 Paris Agreement, signed by Global world leaders including former President Barack Obama. According to the Washington Post, “China, the largest greenhouse gas polluter, has said it plans to reach peak emissions by 2030 and effectively erase its carbon footprint by 2060”.

How to Motivate Your Community!

Share the Commiting Carbon website with your friends and family nationwide. The more information gathered the more accurate our page will be. Through design and environmental awareness we hope to excite our generation to promote positive change.
